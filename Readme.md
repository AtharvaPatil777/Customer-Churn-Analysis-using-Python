# 📊 Customer Churn Analysis & Business Insights using Python

## 📌 Project Overview

Customer churn is one of the biggest challenges for subscription-based businesses. This project analyzes customer churn data from a telecommunications company to identify the key factors influencing customer attrition and provides actionable business recommendations to improve customer retention.

The project covers the complete analytics workflow, including data cleaning, exploratory data analysis (EDA), feature engineering, business insights, and data-driven recommendations.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Customer retention is more cost-effective than acquiring new customers. The objective of this project is to answer the following business questions:

- Which customers are more likely to churn?
- What factors contribute most to customer churn?
- How can the company reduce customer attrition?
- Which customer segments should be targeted for retention campaigns?

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Perform data cleaning and preprocessing.
- Handle missing values and data inconsistencies.
- Conduct exploratory data analysis (EDA).
- Identify patterns and trends affecting customer churn.
- Generate business insights.
- Provide actionable recommendations to improve customer retention.

## 💡 Key Business Insights

- Customers with month-to-month contracts are significantly more likely to churn.
- Customers with shorter tenure have the highest churn rate.
- Fiber Optic customers show relatively higher churn than DSL users.
- Customers paying higher monthly charges are more likely to leave.
- Senior citizens demonstrate a comparatively higher churn rate.
- Electronic Check users experience higher churn than customers using automatic payment methods.

---

## 📈 Business Recommendations

- Promote long-term contracts through loyalty programs and discounts.
- Improve onboarding experience for new customers.
- Review pricing strategies for premium plans.
- Enhance Fiber Optic service quality and customer support.
- Encourage automatic payment methods.
- Develop targeted retention campaigns for high-risk customer segments.
